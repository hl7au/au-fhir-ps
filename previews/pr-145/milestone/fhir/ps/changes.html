Release TBD

  • Publication date: TBD
  • Publication status: TBD
  • Based on FHIR version: 4.0.1

This change log documents the significant updates and resolutions implemented from version 1.0.0-ballot to TBD.

Changes in this version

  • added guidance clarifying expectations for Must Support obligations on elements with multiple cardinality in General Requirements page AU PS: FHIR-53114
  • AU PS MedicationStatement:
    • changed MedicationStatement.medicationCodeableConcept binding to Australian Medication Use (preferred) AU Base: FHIR-53469
    • changed guidance for representing no known medications using SNOMED CT 787481004 |No known medications|, aligned with IPS guidance AU PS: FHIR-52274
  • AU PS Organization:
    • added AU HAE as an allowed type for Organization.identifier AU Base: FHIR-54928
    • added AU HSP-O as an allowed type for Organization.identifier AU Base: FHIR-54923
  • AU PS RelatedPerson:
    • applied technical correction to remove SHOULD:display obligation on RelatedPerson.patient.reference for the AU PS Consumer AU PS: FHIR-56175

Release 1.0.0-ballot

  • Publication date: 2026-02-16
  • Publication status: Ballot for Working Standard
  • Based on FHIR version: 4.0.1

This change log documents the significant updates and resolutions implemented from version 0.3.0-ballot to 1.0.0-ballot. The changes in this update are for ballot.

Changes in this version

  • changed AU PS Consumer guidance to align wording with the handle obligation (replace "consume resources" with "handle") in General Requirements page AU PS: FHIR-52616.
